AI SaaS case study

Research Nova

Academic AI SaaS developed with Solinov Tecnologia using LLM-assisted development under architecture-first execution, secure product flows, quality gates, and production accountability.

Problem
Academic users need faster writing, translation, statistical decisions, and research organization without reducing rigor or traceability.
Role
LLM development focused on architectural decisions, secure flows, quality gates, and production-grade implementation.
Period
Solinov Tecnologia, 08-2024 - 10-2025.

Product Surface

  • AI writing assistant for scientific and academic text workflows.
  • Orthographic and stylistic review for grammar, clarity, cohesion, and academic standards.
  • Academic translation with focus on technical terminology preservation.
  • Statistical analysis classifier and effect size calculators for research decisions.
  • Research management module for organizing projects, documents, and analysis workflows.

Engineering Focus

  • Architecture-first LLM development across feature boundaries, authenticated product flows, and research-oriented modules.
  • AI-assisted execution used as a force multiplier while preserving review discipline, quality gates, and production responsibility.
  • Security and product reliability concerns across login, registration, password recovery, user validation, terms, privacy, and cookies.
  • Feature availability exposed through API-backed enabled modules, supporting controlled rollout of writing, statistics, and research tools.

Observed Public Signals

  • Public landing communicates 10K+ analyzed documents, 1000+ active researchers, 98% precision, and 50x faster review than human workflows.
  • Free and Premium plans indicate a commercial SaaS model with onboarding and account creation flows.
  • Public API exposes enabled product features for writing, translation, statistics, and research management.
